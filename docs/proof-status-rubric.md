## Inequality status

- `valid by aggregation`: obtained as a nonnegative combination of known valid inequalities.
- `valid by c-MIR`: obtained from a documented c-MIR derivation.
- `observed facet`: appears as a facet in a computed small instance.
- `observed redundant`: generated by a family but not facet-defining in tested instances.
- `facetness unproved`: validity may be known, but facet-defining status is not proved.
- `complete hull unproved`: no reverse-inclusion proof has been provided.
